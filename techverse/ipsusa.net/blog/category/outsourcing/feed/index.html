]]> Customer support services are quintessentially an investment that guarantees a company’s profits, brand loyalty, and customer satisfaction in the long run.

What are Customer Support Services?

“Customer support is the range of services you offer to help your customers get the most out of your product and to resolve their problems. Customer support includes things like answering customer questions, providing assistance with onboarding, troubleshooting, and upgrading customers to a new product or service.” (Source: PETTERSEN)

The Dynamics of the Global Customer Support Service Industry

“The global customer experience management market is projected to grow from $11.34 billion in 2022 to $ 32.53 billion in 2029 at a CAGR of 16.2% in the 2022-20229 period.” (Source: INSIGHTS)

The unprecedented growth in customer support outsourcing services is a testament to the value that it adds to businesses. The industry is expanding for the foreseeable future due to the huge impact that the COVID pandemic has had on customer preferences.

Building a Brand in a Post-COVID World

During the COVID pandemic, the entire workspaces were shifted into an online module. Consequently, the entire customer support service became one of the most important aspects of businesses.

Business process outsourcing was one of the few direct human-to-human relationship-building contact points that the firms had with their clients.

“The emergence of digital payment applications such as Google Pay, Amazon Pal, Pay Pal, and others have improved the customer experience amidst the pandemic. These applications have reduced physical interactions and have directed customers toward contactless shopping and payments, thereby supporting enterprises to improve customer experience.” (Source: INSIGHTS)

Moreover, the trend of online shopping is predicted to continue for the years to come, and it seeps deeper into all industries in a post-pandemic world.

Clients prefer communicating with businesses majorly online as it saves them a lot of time and energy that goes into physically doing the same tasks.

Increase Brand Loyalty

As a business owner, the most pertinent goal is to retain clients as that is central to increasing profits and building a brand image. One tool that can help you achieve this goal is professional customer support services, as it tends to build a direct connection between both parties.

“81 percent of consumers say a positive customer service experience increases the likelihood they’ll make a repeat purchase. And 70% say they’ve made purchase decisions based on the quality of customer support.” (Bishop, 2022)

Increase In Sales and Profit Margins

Client support services are effective marketing tools, and it has proved to be highly successful in bringing new clients and retaining existing ones, consequently being extremely effective in increasing profits.

 “71% of leaders report that their agents are essential to driving sales. Customer service leaders should go beyond solving problems and transform their organization by fostering profitable growth.” (Source: Zendesk)

Conclusion

Outsourcing customer support service can drastically improve the profit margins of your organization as it is one of the most effective marketing tools to flourish and reach new markets.

Moreover, having an expert help in BPO customer support services helps to build the firm in the industry in which they operate.

]]> Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) services can serve in many ways to boost business productivity, efficiency, and reliability. Each of the services you acquire aligns with different business activities. For instance, they include IT management, human resource & recruitment, and call center outsourcing, etc. among others.

Two of the terms often used interchangeably but in a similar context are outsourcing and offshoring. Outsourcing means handing over a business process to someone outside of the company; while, offshoring goes with outsourcing companies in a different geographical location, often physically unreachable by the company that hires them.

Furthermore, offshoring means outsourcing business operations in another country. The process of offshoring comes with an immediate benefit of fewer expenses, whether it is the rent of the building or employee expenses. Each of those duties is the responsibility of the company you choose to offshore some of the additional company work.

Not just them, with the right BPO company, businesses can scale without spending a fortune.

What is Offshoring?

Offshoring refers to the contracting of business operations to companies that work in another country. With changing times, business owners understand that to get greater profits and expand the working scale, skillful resources are important. In this context, BPO service providers work the best in a cost-effective manner at a faster pace.

Because of all these features, business owners tend to consult BPO services more than ever to manage non-core tasks. In what ways businesses owe to offshoring, let’s find out!

There are numerous reasons why a company chooses to do professional offshoring.  When done right, businesses get a competitive edge among the rest as follows.

The Advantage of Cost-Efficiency Rules This Industry

Generally, countries where currency value is low, are the mainstream markets of offshoring companies.  In those countries, wages are low. Thus, there is no argument that offshoring saves money.

A task, especially a non-core operation that may require equal dedication of resources as the core operations, can create trouble for the company. Offshoring serves the solution by providing access to skillful human and mechanic resources and is certainly the most attractive benefit.

The Access to Contractual Services

The downsizing factor is eliminated when a company outsources operations from a company of a different region. BPO services or offshoring works on a contractual basis. Businesses get the benefit of hiring skillful resources, however; they can do it for even a short period.

The flexibility that offshoring offers is incredible and can be revamped as per the hiring company’s economic conditions. For Instance, if a company wants software expertise for 6 months, it is immoral to hire someone for 6 months. On the contrary, offshore outsourcing companies work for you for a specified time.

Skillful Experienced Resources Do Your Work

There are some nations where certain industries flourish more. Offshoring facilitates access to those resourceful industries in the budget.

The experienced qualified staff makes a difference in the quality of work. It helps in competing with the already established companies. It makes an ideal environment for the progressive infrastructure of the business.

Enhancement of the Production Quality

Finding the right in-house professional can be a troublesome process. Often, you need to train new recruits and polish them according to the rules and regulations of the company.

However, when hiring offshore outsourcing companies, the investment in training someone vanishes away. The productivity and the quality of work increase automatically and businesses get to improve revenue cycle management.

Company Get Expert in Core Operations

No business can run on its own. It needs to hire people to do operations, and there is no limit to the types of work that require accomplishment. IT, finance, HR, security, logistics, and many more tasks need to be done to run a company.

Offshoring shares the burden of a company and participates in business growth and success. When a company doesn’t have to worry about a variety of works at the same time, it becomes an expert in core competencies. Consequently, profit generation increases, and the overall performance of the company improves.
